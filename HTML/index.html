ExoDiscovery

WELCOME TO EXODISCOVERY!

ExoDiscovery's mission is to provide an interactive online resource that allows anybody, regardless of prior knowledge, to feel as though they are taking part in the discovery of planets that exist outside our solar system.

Don't know what an exoplanet is? Our About page has all the answers, where visitors can explore the methods that astronomers use to detect exoplanet candidates.

Astronomers and data scientists analyze the data collected from the light of nearby stars. Computer algorithms are used to validate whether an exoplanet candidate is in-fact a real planet. Visitors can learn about the most well-known algorithms in the Models page.

Our interactive Catalog provides a never-before-seen repository of exoplanet data, including over 30,000 exoplanet candidates. We have reproduced the results of leading algorthims so that astronomers can distinguish between different exoplanet candidates. ExoDiscovery attempts to further the field of explanet discovery by implementing its own validation model.
