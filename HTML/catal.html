Our Catalog of Exoplanets

Below is our interactive data repository. Users can find exoplanet candidates by their Kepler ID, and view the results of different models. The 'Agreement' column shows whether the different models produce consistent results, and users can filter by model agreement. Hovering over a KEP star filters to all TCEs within that star system, while hovering on a particular TCE brings up its particular light curves. Clicking on specific exoplanet candidates also brings up model results, metadata, and images of their light curves.
